Welcome to the U-Dry Guide!

Here's a simple guide to help you get started with renting and returning umbrellas.

1. Find a Stall

Use the interactive map on our homepage to locate the nearest U-Dry umbrella stall. You can see how many umbrellas are available at each location.

2. Rent an Umbrella

At the stall, select 'Rent Umbrella' on the machine or in the app. Scan the QR code on the machine using your phone's camera through our app to unlock an umbrella. Make sure you have enough balance in your account (min. HK$100).

3. Enjoy Your Rental

Use the umbrella for as long as you need. The first 30 minutes are free, then it's HK$5 per hour. The charge is capped at HK$25 per 24-hour period. Remember to return it within 3 days (72 hours) to avoid forfeiting your deposit.

4. Return the Umbrella

You can return the umbrella to ANY U-Dry stall that has an available slot. In the app, select 'Return Umbrella' and scan the QR code on the return slot of the machine. Ensure the umbrella is securely placed.
